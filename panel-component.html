Standard Panel

The standard panel is the most common layout pattern. It includes a header, body, and optional footer. Panels may be collapsed when sections of content are optional or frequently hidden. Collapsed/Expanded state should persist when possible.

Panel Without Footer

Generic Panel Title

Generic panel information text.

Body content goes here.

Panels do not require a footer. Use this variant when no actions are required. Body padding remains consistent even without a footer. Footer space should not be simulated with empty containers.

Static Panel

Generic Panel Title

Generic panel information text.

Body content goes here.

Panels may be static and not allow collapse/expand behavior when sections of content are important and never collapsed.

Interaction

  • Collapsible panels rotate the chevron 180 degrees between expanded and collapsed states.
  • Body content hides when collapsed.
  • aria-expanded is updated on the toggle control.
  • Hover behavior applies a slightly darker border color.
